SLEEP ANALYZER

Welcome to the Sleep Quality Analyzer, your all-in-one tool for understanding and improving your sleep patterns. Whether you're looking to analyze your sleep quality, predict how well you'll sleep, or even diagnose potential sleep disorders, this platform has got you covered.







How to Use:


User Information: To get started, provide your age, gender, name, and email to personalize your experience.
Data Entry: Specify the number of days to log sleep duration, activity level, and stress level.
Training: Click "Train Model" after entering sleep data for accurate predictions.
Predict Sleep Quality: Estimate future sleep quality by inputting sleep duration, activity level, and stress level.
Sleep Disorder Diagnosis: Answer questions about snoring and nighttime awakenings for sleep health insights.


What It Does:

Analyze Sleep Quality: Identify sleep pattern improvements from your data.
Predictive Modeling: Estimate future sleep quality based on your input and trained model.
Sleep Disorder Diagnosis: Assess common issues like snoring and awakenings.
